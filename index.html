Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my headshot session?

We recommend professional attire that reflects your industry standard. Solid colors work best, avoiding busy patterns or bright colors. Bring 2-3 outfit options to ensure variety. For men, a suit jacket with a solid shirt is ideal. For women, professional blazers or blouses work well.

How long does a typical session take?

Standard sessions last 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses, expressions, and outfit changes. We ensure a relaxed atmosphere where you can feel comfortable and confident throughout the shoot.

What's included in the final delivery?

You'll receive a comprehensive package including 3-5 fully retouched photos in both high-resolution and web-optimized formats, perfect for print and digital use. Additional retouched photos can be purchased separately.

When will I receive my photos?

Your professionally edited photos will be delivered within 3-5 business days via a secure digital gallery. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer group corporate rates?

Yes, we offer special packages for corporate teams of 5 or more. These can be scheduled on-site at your office for convenience. Contact us for custom quotes and availability.
